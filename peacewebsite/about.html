Our Story

We are continually seeking new methods to convey the mission of Islam. Consequently, this educational platform was established. Through the dedication of our Quran services, hundreds and thousands of individuals in Pakistan and many other regions of the world have gained proficiency in Quran recitation. Peace Online Quran Academy is fulfilling its responsibilities to spread Islamic teachings globally under the guidance of Maulana Sheikh Muhammad Ilyas Qadri, and this platform stands as a recent significant achievement, with the primary aim of educating people of all ages worldwide regarding the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah.

Through his sincere efforts, by the mercy of Allah and the blessings of His beloved Prophet, he has successfully enhanced the enthusiasm of young and old, men and women alike, to learn the Quran and to embed a noble Madani vision in the hearts of Muslims. Viewing this as a great mission, Sheikh established Dawat-e-Islami, an international, non-political, Sunni Barelwi revivalist movement of Aashqan-e-Rasool (The Lovers of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ) in Zul-Qa‘da-tul-Haram in 1981, which now has hundreds of thousands of followers in Pakistan and more than 180 other countries around the globe.

The movement concentrates on spreading Islamic knowledge worldwide and operates two primary services: Jamia-tul-Madina (a network of religious institutions) and the non-commercial Madani TV channel. He has authored more than 30 books, including Faizan-e-Sunnat, which highlights the virtues of righteous deeds. He remains continuously dedicated to disseminating the message of Islam across the world in every sphere of life.
